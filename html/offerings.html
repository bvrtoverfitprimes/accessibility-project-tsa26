Offerings

What we build and explore

We currently provide two core solutions designed to enhance digital accessibility immediately.

Live Captions

A downloadable Live Captions tool that runs locally (Windows demo). It listens to system audio and displays captions in a small always-on-top window.

Magnification

A downloadable Magnification tool that runs locally (Windows demo). It provides a lens/bar overlay that magnifies whatever is under your cursor while you use other apps.
