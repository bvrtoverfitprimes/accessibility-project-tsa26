Explore small, interactive prototypes that demonstrate how accessibility tools can work in real time.

Circle Magnification

This tool is a simple circular magnifier that enlarges anything directly underneath it. Move your cursor over the demo to see the lens follow your pointer.

Use this for quick, precise focus—like reading small UI labels, icons, or dense text without changing the page layout.

Bar / Line Magnification

This magnifier behaves like a reading bar: it stretches across the screen from left to right and magnifies a horizontal slice of content.

It’s especially helpful for reading long documents (legal text, books, research) because you can keep your place while zooming in without losing context.

Live Captions

Many videos online don't include captions or subtitles. The downloadable Live Captions tool transcribes system audio in real time using Whisper.
